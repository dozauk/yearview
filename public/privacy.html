Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What YearView does

YearView is a self-hosted web application that displays your Google Calendar events in a year view. It is operated for personal use and does not collect, store, or sell your data to third parties.

Google account access

YearView requests read-only access to your Google Calendar (calendar.readonly scope) and your email address (userinfo.email) for identification. It cannot create, edit, or delete your calendar events.

Data storage

  • Your OAuth tokens are stored server-side in an encrypted session (SQLite), never in the browser.
  • Calendar event data is fetched on demand and held only in your browser's memory for the current session — it is not written to any database.
  • No analytics, tracking scripts, or third-party cookies are used.

Data retention

Sessions expire after 7 days of inactivity. You can revoke access at any time via Google Account Permissions or by signing out.
